Kebberfegg is a tool for making keyword-based RSS feeds.

Start by entering a query that you want to monitor as a keyword-based RSS feed.



When you're ready to make fresh feeds, just reload the page to reset the check boxes.

RSS Feeds Available

Tick a box and the URL for an RSS feed will appear. Beef up your feed reader!
